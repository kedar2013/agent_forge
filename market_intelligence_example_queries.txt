Agent Forge — MARKET INTELLIGENCE AGENT FAMILY
Showcase-quality example queries for testing / demoing each specialist
(Try these in /chat, or in the Playground at /agents/<id>/playground)
==============================================================================

0. SHOWCASE — the best single queries to demo the app's depth
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- I want to start an SIP of 10,000 rupees a month. Based on historical data
  and current trends, which sectors should I broadly look at?
    -> real sector-by-sector performance table (3M/6M/1Y/3Y), ranked, with
       an honest "sector leadership rotates" caveat — not a stock tip.
- Give me a thorough analysis and prediction for Nippon India Small Cap Fund.
    -> trailing returns AND a real statistical trend/volatility/NAV
       projection (6mo/12mo), clearly labeled as a projection, not a promise.
- If I invest 15,000/month for 5 years in HDFC Small Cap Fund, what could
  that grow to?
    -> a real compound-interest SIP projection based on the fund's own
       trailing CAGR — total invested vs. projected value vs. projected gain.
- Tell me about Infosys — overview, financials, analyst sentiment, and
  recent filings.
    -> pulls together company profile, fundamentals, quarterly earnings,
       analyst upgrades/downgrades, and real SEC filing links in one answer.
- Give me a comparison between Nippon India Small Cap and Quant Small Cap
  fund.
    -> resolves both scheme codes itself and returns a clean side-by-side
       markdown table — no back-and-forth needed.
- Compare gold to the S&P 500 this year.
    -> shows the app's honest limits: answers the part it can and says
       plainly what it doesn't cover, instead of guessing.

2. STOCK_MARKET_ANALYST (stocks, ETFs, indices, and company research)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- What's Apple's stock price right now, and how has it performed this year?
- What's the current price of the Nifty 50 index, and the Nifty 100 too?
- Compare Reliance Industries' 1-month and 1-year returns.
- Tell me about Tesla as a company — what do they do, and what's their
  market cap and P/E ratio?
- What's Microsoft's revenue trend over the last 4 quarters?
- How has Infosys done on quarterly earnings — beating or missing estimates?
- What's the current analyst sentiment on Nvidia — are they mostly bullish?
- Any recent SEC filings for Apple?
- What's the latest news on Tata Motors?
- Give me a full research summary on Amazon — profile, fundamentals,
  earnings, sentiment, and news, all together.

3. CRYPTO_ANALYST (cryptocurrency — CoinGecko)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- What's Bitcoin trading at right now, and how has it done over the past
  6 months?
- What coins are trending right now?
- Give me Ethereum's price and market cap in INR.
- Compare Bitcoin's and Solana's trailing 1-year returns.
- What's Bitcoin's 52-week high and low?

4. FOREX_METALS_ANALYST (currency exchange + precious metals)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- What's the USD to INR exchange rate today, and how has it trended this
  year?
- Convert 2,000 USD to INR.
- What's the current spot price for gold and silver?
- Has the dollar gotten stronger or weaker against the rupee this year?
- Give me spot prices for all four precious metals you track.

5. FUND_ANALYST_AGENT (Indian mutual funds — deep analysis & predictions)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- How has the HDFC Small Cap Fund performed over the last 5 years?
- Give me a thorough analysis and prediction for Quant Small Cap Fund —
  include volatility and a 12-month outlook.
- What's the maximum drawdown Parag Parikh Flexi Cap Fund has seen recently?
- Compare Nippon India Small Cap and Quant Small Cap on both historical
  performance and projected trend.
- I'm considering an SIP of 5,000/month for 10 years in an index fund —
  project what that could become.
- Based on current trends, which sectors have led over the last year, and
  which have lagged?
- Search for HDFC's small cap fund options and tell me the scheme codes.

NOTES
- Every specialist responds with data/analysis only — they're instructed not
  to give buy/sell/hold investment advice, and will say so plainly if asked
  directly (e.g. "should I buy Bitcoin?" or "which fund should I pick?").
- Trend/volatility/SIP projections from fund_analyst_agent are real compound-
  interest math run on real historical NAV data — always presented with an
  explicit "this is a projection, not a guarantee" caveat.
- crypto_analyst's trend analysis is limited to a 1-year lookback (CoinGecko's
  free tier doesn't allow longer daily history without a paid API key).
- Company research coverage is honest about its limits: competitors, ESG
  scores, and credit ratings have no reliable free data source and the agent
  will say so rather than guess — see "application onboarding example.txt"
  for the full breakdown.
- All data comes from free, no-API-key sources: Yahoo Finance, CoinGecko,
  frankfurter.app (ECB rates), gold-api.com, and mfapi.in (AMFI NAV data).
- A single conversation can now move between all of these specialists
  without getting stuck on one — ask about a fund, then crypto, then a
  stock, all in the same chat.
